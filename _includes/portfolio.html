Course Structure

The course mixes mathematics, programming and pure data science to create a balanced skill set.

First Year

Study the fundamentals: mathematics, statistics, programming, and get a taste of the excitement of data analytics.

Second Year

Adds more data and more problem solving, including machine learning, visualisation, and data mining.

Third Year

You will put these fundamentals to work on large data sets, on complex problems, and one real-world scenarios in our unique data science ‘pods’, where you will work with enterprises and cultural institutions on their data and with their people. The second half of third year is dedicated to an INTRA, where you will join a Data Science team in a large, data-driven organisation and gain real understanding of your future role

Fourth Year

You will bring your skills and learning to tackle creative and practical solutions to complex problems. This will include more ‘pods’, the latest developments in programming, mathematics and data science, and a significant individual project showcasing your potential to the many interested employers.

All students graduating from this NFQ Level 8 Honours Bachelors awards will receive the degree of Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Data Science (BSc DS).
